MagCalc makes dealing with magnetic units easy!

Check out what you can do with this app!

Convert

Choose a magnetic property and convert your unit of choice into many other units on the fly.

Calculate

By knowing the density and/or molar mass, you can, for instance, calculate the volume magnetisation from mass magnetisation and vice versa.

Demagnetisation

Calculate the demagnetising factor for certain sample shapes in order to correct your magnetic measurements.

Periodic table

Use the periodic table to get compacted infos on each element or to obtain the molar mass of a compound.
