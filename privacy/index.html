Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs your use of the software application "MagCalc" for mobile devices created by Rolf Stinshoff and the associated product's website. This Privacy Policy was last updated on April 9, 2019.

Data handling on the use of MagCalc

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The application does not collect or transmit any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) about you, such as your name, address, phone number or email address.

How do you handle location data?

The application does not use or collect any data related to your geographic location.

Can users see their personal data?

The application itself does not collect, transmit or maintain user data.

Do you share personal information?

As no personal information is collected, transmitted, or maintained by the application, no personal information is shared with anyone.

Do advertising companies collect data?

The application has no facility for collecting, transmitting, or maintaining user data, so no data is shared with advertising companies.

Do you use vendors or analytics providers?

No. The application has no facility for collecting, transmitting, or maintaining user data, so no data is shared with vendors or analytics providers.

Do you store any kind of user data?

The application stores user default settings about the display or dismissal of quick guides and info texts in order to be able to restore these settings. This data is only locally stored on the device as long as the application is installed on your device. This data also can't be accessed outside of the application. No other data on how you use the application, e.g. which values you are typing in, is collected, transmitted or maintained by the application.
